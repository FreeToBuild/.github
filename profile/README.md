## The Movement

**Free to Build** is a global revolution to restore the most basic human right that every other animal on Earth already enjoys: the freedom to claim unclaimed or long-abandoned land and build your own shelter — **without permits, licenses, fees, or government approval**.

No more begging for permission to exist.
No more regulations that make housing unaffordable.
No more treating land like it belongs only to those who can pay or navigate bureaucracy.

We believe **building a home is not a privilege — it is a birthright**.

## Article 31 — The Right to Build Freely

The 1948 Universal Declaration of Human Rights affirms the right to property (Art. 17) and to housing (Art. 25). It does not yet protect the single act that makes those rights real — claiming unclaimed land and building shelter on it. We propose one more article:

> **Article 31.** Everyone has the inherent right to claim unclaimed or long-abandoned land and to construct a dwelling or shelter for themselves and their family thereon — without permit, license, fee, or prior authorization. This right is a fundamental expression of human dignity and a natural extension of the rights to life, liberty, security of person, property, and an adequate standard of living.

Full proposal with commentary: [`docs/proposed-udhr-amendment.md`](https://github.com/FreeToBuild/docs/blob/main/proposed-udhr-amendment.md).

## Core Principles

- **Freedom to Build** — No permits. No licenses. No fees. Just build.
- **Claim Unclaimed Land** — If it’s truly unowned and unused, it belongs to whoever stands on it and improves it.
- **No Housing Regulations** — Your shelter, your rules. Safety is your responsibility.
- **Hope for All** — This movement is for everyone: the broke, the young, the displaced, the adventurous, the families who just want a roof they own.
- **Peaceful Revolution** — We build, we document, we share, we grow. No violence. Just unstoppable creation.
